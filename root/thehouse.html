The House

182 6th Street NW

The current Kappa Sigma Fraternity House was finished in October of 2013, making it the newest house on Georgia Tech's campus. It has 25 total bedrooms and can house 49 brothers comfortably. Each 'cubby' has a lofted bed, desk, dresser, and clothes rack.

Some of the accomodations include a commercial-grade kitchen, TV Room, Brotherhood lounge, Study room, and the Pit for a wide variety of fraternal activities.
